About Us

​Shenzhen Creality 3D technology which was founded in 2014, is one of the first group of enterprises devoting to the 3D printing in China.
Creality 3D focus on 3D printing research, development, manufacturing and sales. We also supply printer accessories, consumables, educations, printing services and etc. At the present time, Creality 3D has become one of the best sales companies in 3D printing industry.

Creality 3D pursue innovation, and struggle for 3D printing industry. From early stages to the future of our company, we continue innovating to provide customers cost-effective 3D printers.
As fact it is, in the year 2014, we sold 3000 3D printers in the year 2015,we have sold more than 10000 printers.

Our company has invested huge amount of money to build a large standard production line, which can provide 60000 printers per year. As one of the high technology manufacturing enterprises, we have a strong R&D strength as an example, we have dozens of independent intellectual property rights and patents for our inventions.
Our products are CE, FCC, ROHS certified. We also export our products to Europe, America, Australia, Russia, England , Germany ,Singapore ,Egypt, India and​ other countries.
